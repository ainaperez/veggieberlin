Brammibal's - The best vegan donuts

  • Kreuzberg
  • Breakfast

Order online

www.brammibalsdonuts.com

24.03.2021

Brammibal's Donuts is home to the best vegan donuts in Berlin. It's by far one of my favourite places for a sweet treat and a coffee. With several shops around the city and a continuous supply of new creations, it's impossible not to visit them several times in the month

×

It began in 2015, when they started selling donuts in markets and finally opened their first shop in Maybachufer. Now you can find them in 5 more locations: Potsdamer platz, KaDeWe, Warschauer Str, Rosa-Luxemburg-Straße (near Alexanderplatz) or up in Prenzlauer Berg.

They offer a wide variety of permanent donuts but I love their monthly specilaities, which range from decadent and chocolaty creations in the winter months to more fruity and colourful mixes in the summer months. My favourite so far included the biscoff cream donut, the Bienenstich, or the past March's nougat & pistachio donut. And my all time choice, the peanut butter and jelly donut, which you can enjoy during all summer

They offer as well weekend specials, which make it impossible to refuse the temptation to visit them every weekend. That's marketing done right.

This pasts months they have also introduced the Monday Cinammon buns, which you can enjoy, you guessed it, every monday. You can also pair them with their coffee, brought by the local coffee roasters Populus.

You will apreciate their use of local suppliers and their compromise with the enviroment and causes. Every month they destinate 1€, from every charity donut sold, to a designated charity. As far as I know, they were able to donate aroun 90.000€ in 2020. At least some good news for that hell of a year.

Make sure to visit them soon, or check their Instagram to see their new releases
